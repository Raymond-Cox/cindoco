  • Hook N' Loop backing which can be attached to interface pad or directly to conversion pad
  • New Wave Discs are patented design which features wavy edge around disc
  • Can be used with dual action finishing sanders
  • Wavy edge extends over edge of backup pad which protects it and makes it last longer
  • Ideal for finishing inside radius corners, coves, and molds
Available in 5" and 6" diameters, as well as 80G, 120G, and 180G

Hi-Per PSA Sanding Discs

  • Heavy "C" weight paper discs are made from aluminum oxide with non-loading stearate
  • Pressure Sensitive Adhesive (PSA) backing with protective release liner on every disc
  • Ideal for finishing sanding of woods, paints, metals, and plastics
Available in 5" and 6" diameters, as well as 80G, 120G, and 180G

Interface & Conversion Pads

  • Made from rubber material
  • Placed between the sanding disc and backup pad
  • Adds more padding and protects backup pad by placing more space between the sanding surface and the sander itself
  • Converts PSA backup pad to Hook N' Loop pad
  • Conversion pad allows users to switch between sanding discs of different grits when working on a project
Available in 5" and 6" diameters

Sanding Sheets

  • 9" x 11" sanding sheets
  • Non-loading paper
  • Excellent for workshops
Available in 80G, 120G, and 180G

Wavy Edge Scuff & Buff Ball

  • Wavy edge allows ball to reach into tight spaces
  • Power scuffing replaces labor intensive scuffing
  • Wavy edge design lasts longer due to renewing scuffing surface
  • Works with most drills and electric or pneumatic grinders

QUILT HANGERS

Cindoco Wood Products and its Craftwood Division are pleased to offer a line of very useful products specifically targeting quilters.

The Quilt Hanger is a wood hanger with a marble which allows you to hang your quilts or tapestries without putting holes in them. When you push the quilt into the slot, the marble drops down and gravity holds the quilt securely in place. To remove, you simply pull up and then to the side.

Cedar Mailboxes & Posts

Cindoco Wood Products Co. is excited to announce the release of two great new products; the CedarBox and CedarPost! Both are handmade in the U.S.A. from Aromatic Red Cedar. The CedarBox is a rustproof poly mailbox covered with a naturally weather resistant red cedar cover. It is fully assembled and ready to install with pre-drilled holes for mounting. Mounting board and hardware are included.

To complete the set, we also offer the CedarPost, which is a mailbox post kit to match the CedarBox. Also made of Aromatic Red Cedar, the CedarPost kit comes with everything you need to effortlessly install your new mailbox. No digging is necessary! All you have to do it just hammer the metal C-beam into the ground, attach the cedar post to the metal C-beam, screw on the back board, and then attach the mailbox on top.

This is a great way to enhance the aesthetics of your home without investing a lot of time or money! For more information on these durable, American made products please contact us today! Display racks available upon request.
